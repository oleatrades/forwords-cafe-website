About Us – Forwords Library Café

Discover the story behind our cozy sanctuary where books meet coffee and friendships flourish.

The Beginning of a Beautiful Friendship

In the heart of Kochi, where tradition meets modern life, Forwords Library Café was born from friendship and a shared dream: to create a space where people could slow down, connect, and feel at home.

This is not just a café. It's a library, a workspace, a hangout spot, and a little retreat for those who believe in savoring the moment — one sip, one page, one thought at a time.

What We Offer

Books to Read

Shelves filled with stories, ideas, and inspiration. Come for coffee, stay for the words.

Pure Arabica Coffee

We prepare our coffee using premium 100% Arabica coffee powder. It's rich, smooth, and more costly than regular blends, but worth every sip.

Freshly Made Food

Nothing is prepared early and stored away. Every dish is made only after you order, so it may take a little more time — but that's how freshness should be.

Space to Work & Relax

Whether you're a freelancer, a student, or simply someone looking for quiet comfort, our cozy corners and free Wi-Fi make working or reading here a pleasure.

Why Forwords?

At Forwords, we believe:

  • Good food and drinks should never be rushed.
  • Great conversations happen when people feel at ease.
  • Coffee and books are best enjoyed slowly.
  • A café should be more than just tables and chairs — it should be a community.

Our Promise

When you walk into Forwords, you'll always find:

  • A warm, welcoming atmosphere.
  • Food and drinks that are made with care, not shortcuts.
  • A place where creativity, comfort, and connection come first.

Forwords isn't just where you eat or drink — it's where friendships begin, ideas grow, and time slows down.
